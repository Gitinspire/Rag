3. DIAGNOSTICS

5. SYNTHETIC BIOLOGY

6. EPIGENETICS

     Multi-Cohort Clinical Research

HOW IT WORKS (4-step workflow):
Step 1 — Upload Data:
  Ingest omics, chemistry, clinical, or instrument data from multiple sources.

Step 2 — AI Analysis:
  Apply advanced models including GNNs (Graph Neural Networks), transformers,
  and domain-specific AI.

Step 3 — Predict & Rank:
  Identify and prioritize hits, variants, biomarkers, and anomalies.

Step 4 — Visualize & Report:
  Generate publication-ready visualizations and regulatory-grade reports.
