CATEGORY: Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1: What does your AI & ML platform offer?
A: The platform delivers advanced AI and machine learning solutions tailored for
   pharmaceutical research and healthcare innovation. It helps organizations accelerate
   drug discovery, analyze complex datasets, automate workflows, and make faster,
   data-driven decisions using predictive models and intelligent automation.

Q2: How does AI help in pharmaceutical research?
A: AI automates complex data analysis tasks, identifies patterns in large biological
   datasets (genomics, proteomics, metabolomics), predicts drug-target interactions,
   and streamlines clinical trial data analysis — dramatically reducing time-to-insight
   for R&D teams.

Q3: Are your solutions customizable?
A: Yes. InnovLabs.ai offers custom algorithm development, custom AI solutions, and
   tailored consultation services to meet specific research and industry requirements.

Q4: Do you provide end-to-end AI implementation?
A: Yes. Services span from data integration and model training to deployment, onboarding,
   training, and 24/7 technical support.

Q5: How can we get started with your services?
A: Teams can request a demo at https://innovlabs.ai or join the beta program. Contact
   via info@innovlabs.ai or call +1 (888) 722-2825.

Q6: What industries do you work with?
A: Primary focus: pharmaceutical R&D, biotech, healthcare, clinical genomics, cancer
   research, synthetic biology, diagnostics, epigenetics, and medical devices.

Q7: How do you ensure data security and compliance?
A: InnovLabs.ai is ISO Certified, AICPA SOC 2 compliant, HIPAA compliant, and GDPR
   compliant. User-owned data is processed in read-only mode. No vendor lock-in.

Q8: Can your solutions integrate with existing systems?
A: Yes. The platform supports data integration from multiple sources including omics,
   chemistry, clinical, and instrument data via open standards without vendor lock-in.
